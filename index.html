The art engine

Not a static mint. A lifecycle.

OnePlantArt combines deterministic seed genetics, genus morphology, mutation classes, palette systems, habitats, stage progression, and sealed-card presentation into one browser-readable on-chain art object.

Variation math

3,025,152 plant-body identities before time moves.

The production identity space combines 78 genus values, 101 epithets, 8 mutation classes, 12 palettes, and 4 cycle phases. Macro Stage and Growth Progress are excluded from this base count.

Living lifecycle

3,630,182,400 stage-aware appearances.

Each identity can travel through 12 Macro Stages and 100 Growth Progress points per stage. Including the 2,147,483,647-value render seed, the procedural render space reaches about 7.79 quintillion stage-aware states.

On-chain craft

100+ contracts for one visual engine.

The NFT renderer is split across layers, groups, kernels, and external shards so the art can stay rich on-chain. This is NOT pixel-like art; it is procedural SVG botany.

Market primitive

Built around Uniswap V4 Hook mechanics.

Official swap flow uses a Uniswap V4 Hook path for UP fee logic, release pacing, burn behavior, and OP mint integration alongside the planting sale.

Time-grown lifecycle

One identity, many moments

The growth atlas shows a single plant moving from seed to mature bloom. It is the clearest way to explain that OP is time-driven art, not a frozen profile picture.

Mature specimens

Sixty finished plants

A quick wall of final-form cards showing how far the same renderer can stretch across structure, color, habitat, and bloom behavior.

Genus library

Seventy-eight visual families

The genus sheet is the strongest diversity proof: grasses, blooms, fungi, trees, pods, aquatic forms, and stranger botanical silhouettes.

Stage coverage

Full lifecycle range

The full-stage sheet shows the engine across early, mid, mature, sealed, sparse, dense, bright, and nocturnal states.
